Donkey

Your Mac, on autopilot.

What it is

Donkey is a general-purpose desktop agent for macOS. Describe a task in plain language and it drives your apps, runs shell workflows, edits media, fills PDFs, and browses the web to get it done.

Features

  • Drives any app through accessibility and vision
  • Media: download, trim, transcribe, and subtitle video
  • Documents: fill PDF forms, convert images, crunch spreadsheets
  • Web: search, scrape to markdown, and run multi-step flows

Pricing

Free during the open beta.
