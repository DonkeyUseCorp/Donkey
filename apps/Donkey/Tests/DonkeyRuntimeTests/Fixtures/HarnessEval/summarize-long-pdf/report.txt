Q3 PLATFORM RELIABILITY REVIEW

Executive summary: availability was 99.92%, below the 99.95% target, driven by two
incidents in the payments path. Mean time to recovery improved 18% quarter over quarter.

Section 1 — Incidents. INC-204 (payments timeout, 47 min) and INC-219 (cache stampede,
31 min) accounted for 78 of 83 minutes of downtime. Both traced to missing backpressure.

Section 2 — Capacity. Peak traffic grew 22%. Database CPU headroom fell to 15% at peak;
read-replica rollout is recommended before Q4 promotions.

Section 3 — Action items. Add backpressure to the payments queue, ship read replicas,
and add a cache-stampede guard. Owners assigned; target dates in Q4.
