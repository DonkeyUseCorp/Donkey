Attention Routing in Sparse Models

Abstract
We study how routing decisions in sparse mixture-of-experts models affect downstream
accuracy. We find that load-balancing losses trade capacity for stability.

1. Introduction
Sparse models activate a subset of parameters per token. Routing is the mechanism that
chooses which experts handle which tokens.

2. Method
We add an auxiliary balance term and measure expert utilization across layers.

3. Results
Balanced routing improves validation accuracy by 1.4 points at equal compute.
